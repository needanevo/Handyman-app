Frequently Asked Questions

What are your fees?

We charge 15% of the job total. That's it. No monthly fees, no lead charges, no hidden costs. You keep 85% of every dollar you earn.

Do I need to be licensed in all states?

No. You only need a valid license in the state(s) where you operate. We verify your license jurisdiction and only show you jobs in your licensed areas.

What types of jobs will I see?

Jobs requiring licensed contractors: electrical work, HVAC installations, major plumbing, structural work, roofing, etc. Customers who specifically want licensed, insured professionals.

How does payment work?

Customer's payment is held in escrow when they accept your proposal. Once you complete the work and customer confirms, payment is released to your bank account via direct deposit.

Can I bring my own crew?

Absolutely. You manage your own crew and subcontractors. You're an independent business‚Äîwe just connect you with customers.

What if there's a dispute?

We have a dispute resolution process. If customer and contractor can't agree on completion, our support team mediates based on job description, photos, and communications.

How do I verify my license?

During signup, you'll upload photos of your license, insurance certificate, and business registration. We verify with state databases and notify you within 2-3 business days.
