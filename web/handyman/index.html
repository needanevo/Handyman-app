Frequently Asked Questions

Do I need a license to start?

No. You can start as a handyman with no license. As you grow, you can get licensed to qualify for bigger jobs and higher rates.

How much does it cost to join?

Joining is free. We only charge a 15% fee when you complete a job. No monthly fees, no hidden costs.

How do I get paid?

Payment is released to your bank account via direct deposit after the customer confirms the job is complete. Payouts are processed within 3-5 business days.

What types of jobs can I do?

Anything you're skilled at: plumbing repairs, electrical work, carpentry, painting, drywall, HVAC, flooring, landscaping, and more. You choose which job requests to respond to.

Can I set my own prices?

Yes! Customers post jobs with estimated budgets. You submit proposals with your pricing and timeline. You're in control.

Do I need insurance?

Not required to start as a handyman, but highly recommended. As you grow, having insurance helps you win more jobs and protects you and your customers.
